Person of Interest

♡ Maya Angelou ♡

Maya Angelou was an American poet, novelist, and civil rights activist. She was born on April 4, 1928, in St. Louis, Missouri. Maya Angelou's life and work continue to inspire millions around the world.

With her powerful words and storytelling, Maya Angelou touched the hearts of people from all walks of life. Her autobiographical book, "I Know Why the Caged Bird Sings," is considered a modern classic, and her poetry collections, such as "And Still I Rise," resonate with strength, resilience, and hope. My personal favorite of her collection is the book "Letter to my Daughter", addressed to a daugther she never really had, but intended for female readers around the world, the book is a compilation of many of Angelou's insightful life experiences from being a young girl - to travelling the world, all with something valuable to learn from.

"You may encounter many defeats, but you must not be defeated. In fact, it may be necessary to encounter the defeats, so you can know who you are, what you can rise from, how you can still come out of it."
